About Me Read in Malayalam

Dr.Biju Ananthakrishnan(Shivayogi) is an Indian Music Director, Singer, Lyricist, Orchestrator, Concert arranger, playback artist, Programmer and Filmmaker. His best-renowned compositions in Malayalam films are Akashangalkkappuram (2015), Nimisham (2017), Audi Plus, AkaleOral, Colony and the upcoming movie Theatre.

He has directed music for more than 500 Drama and tele films, Documentary Film Audio & Video Album, home cinema over 5000 songs in Malayalam, Tamil, Kannada and Hindi ghazals which include devotional songs and others
